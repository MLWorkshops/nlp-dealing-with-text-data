place sparerib in a pot, and cover with water.
place pot over high heat, and bring to a boil.
low heat slightly, and cook, cover, for 15 or 20 minute, then remove rib to a large bowl and discard the water.
while the rib cook, pour the oil into a dutch oven set over high heat, and swirl it around.
season the chicken thigh with salt and pepper, and when the oil begin to shimmer and be about to smoke, add them to the pan, skinside down.
Brown the chicken aggressively, about 5 to 7 minute per side, then add the chicken to the bowl with the rib.
repeat with the sausage, brown them on all side for approximately 5 to 7 minute, then add to the bowl with the chicken and the rib.
cook the onion and garlic in the fat remain in the dutch oven, stir occasionally until they soften and begin to brown, approximately 5 to 7 minute.
heat oven to 300.
add the tomato paste, vinegar, lemon juice, hotpepper sauce, chicken stock, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, chile powder and paprika to the onion and garlic in the dutch oven, then stir to combine, and allow to cook for 1 to 2 minute.
add the meat to the dutch oven, stir and toss them so that they be well cover with the sauce, then cover the pot, and place in the oven to cook, undisturbed, for approximately 90 minute, or until the meat be tender and, in the case of the chicken and the rib, pull from the bone.
